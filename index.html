About Me

I am a full stack developer who has a passion for creating tools that improve the lives of ordinary people.

I previously worked as both a quality assurance lead and senior product specialist at One Medical. I am well versed in the structure of electronic medical record applications and have working knowledge of Meaningful Use Certification, HL7, ICD-10, CPT, SNOMED, and UMLS.

I live in New York City, am originally from the San Francisco Bay Area, and am always up for a new adventure. I look forward to working with you and your team!
